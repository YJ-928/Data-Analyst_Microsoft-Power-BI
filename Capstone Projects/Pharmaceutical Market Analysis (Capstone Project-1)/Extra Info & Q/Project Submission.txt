Project Submission

1. MCQ Test
The questions in this MCQ are already given to you in the cheat sheet. Please refer to the cheat sheet and prepare the report accordingly such that you can comfortably answer all questions. After this is done, only then attend this MCQ.

2. Project Explanation Video
 Once you have successfully completed the MCQ test, the next step is to create a project explanation video which is mandatory. In this video, you will have to show your project, explaining your approach, methodology, challenges faced, and the key insights derived. The video should not exceed 10 minutes and must be clear, concise, and well-organized.

3. Steps to upload the video
Once the project explanation video has been recorded, upload your video to your drive and give access to everyone then share the video link in the submission form.
