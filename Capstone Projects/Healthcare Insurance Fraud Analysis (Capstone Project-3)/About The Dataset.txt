About the Dataset
This dataset has 4 CSV files elaborating the details of Healthcare Insurance.

InPatientData.csv : The claims filed for those patients who are admitted in the hospitals.

OutPatientData.csv : The claims filed for those patients who visit hospitals and not admitted in it.

Beneficiary.csv : Beneficiary KYC (Know Your Customer) details like health conditions, region, etc.

GeninueOrFraud.csv : Whether each insurance provider is genuine or fraudulent.

Column_Descriptions.xlsx : The columns of all tables in the dataset. (No need to load this file into your visualization tool, it is just for understanding the dataset.)
